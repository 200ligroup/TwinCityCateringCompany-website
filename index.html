About US

Twin City Catering Company

Twin City Catering Company is the premier catering company in the Twin Cities area, specializing in creating memorable dining experiences for all types of events. Founded by passionate chef Shanta Faison, Twin City Catering Company brings a unique blend of flavors, creativity, and professionalism to every catering event.

With over 20 years of combined experience in the culinary industry, Shanta have honed her skills and perfected her craft, creating innovative and delicious menus that cater to a variety of tastes and dietary preferences. From intimate dinner parties to large corporate events, Twin City Catering Company can cater to any occasion with impeccable service and attention to detail.
